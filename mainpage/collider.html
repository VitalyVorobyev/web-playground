Accelerator Complex

SCT factory is a symmetric electron-positron collider consisting of the following installations:

  1. Positron injector with cooling storage ring
  2. Injector of polarized electrons
  3. Linear accelerator, which accelerates/decelerates the beams to the energy of the experiment
  4. Double ring collider

Collider

The collider has the following features:

  • high single-bunch luminosity due to the Crab Waist collision scheme with large Piwinski angle and submillimeter vertical beta function at the interaction point;
  • multi-bunch regime, which implies the use of the double ring scheme;
  • the final focus on the basis of superconducting quadrupole lenses, providing a small beta function;
  • small horizontal and vertical beam emittances;
  • high intensity of a short bunch (âˆ¼10 mm);
  • effective control of the damping parameters (emittance, damping time, etc.) to ensure maximum luminosity in the entire energy range;
  • high level of longitudinal polarization of the electron beam in the entire energy range;
  • continuous injection at the energy of the experiment, ensuring maintenance of high current, and obtaining a high integral luminosity;
  • simultaneous operation of two high-performance injectors: the source of positrons and the source of polarized electrons.
