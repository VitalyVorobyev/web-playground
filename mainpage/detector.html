Detector concept

SCT detector is a universal particle detector as shown in Fig. 1. It should meet certain requirements derived from the physics program. Some of them are:

  • Momentum resolution better than 0.3%
  • Hermiticity of at least 95% of the full solid angle
  • Soft tracks reconstruction with \(p_t\) starting from 50 MeV
  • Excellent charged particle identification, including \(\pi\)/\(\mu\) separation at \(5\sigma\) level
  • Good \(\gamma\)/\(\pi^0\) separation in the energy range from 10 MeV to 3000 MeV
  • DAQ rate of 300 kHz @ \(J/\psi\) peak
